About MAKALLA Bright Stars

Discover more about Makalla Bright Stars, the home of the little stars.

Our Story

Makalla Bright Stars is a Gauteng based organisation that was born out of a shared frustration and promise to show up to every children in need. We noticed too many children in our communities missing school, lacking school essentials and not coping well in their homes.

We begain in 2021 without funding or formal offices. First gatherings were on an uneven field with six children, built around one commitment: to show up every tuesday and thursday without a fail. After each session we sat together and asked what had made school difficult, the responses were rarely about abiity but about transport costs,lack of school essenials and too many responsiblities at home

Today Makalla Bright Stars run programms in three Gauteng communities, however our approach has not changed. We use sports to bring young people together, learning to keep them grounded and practical skills to help them move foward.

What We Do

We work with kids and teens in our communities who are at risk of falling behind. We use sports to bring them together three times a week. We run after school study session Monday to Thursday and we track school attendence along with Progress. Once a month we host skills saturday where our local adults teach practical things like budgeting, first aid and writing cv, during school holidays we run full-day programmes with tournaments, talent shows and a meal for every child.

Our Mission

Makalla Bright Stars exists to make sure no child in our Gauteng communities is overlooked. We keep young peopele in school, off the streets and connected to real opportunities by show up consistently.

Our Vision

A Gauteng community where every child is seen, supported and equipped to stay in school and shape their own future
